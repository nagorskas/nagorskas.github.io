Light of the Past (2022–2023) is a photography series documenting abandoned and long-forgotten objects, possibly in their final moments before they might demolished or lost to time, with natural processes overtaking them and the man-made structures being reclaimed by nature. The subjects are photographed in the night, receding into the black background and slowly disintegrating in the viewer's eyes before disappearing forever, never to be seen again.
This series expands upon the ideas previously explored in the series Light of the Past: Pioneer Camp (2022).
