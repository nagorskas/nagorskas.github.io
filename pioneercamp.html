Light of the Past: Pioneer Camp (2022) is a photography series documenting an abandoned, Soviet-era pioneer camp found in the woods of Kauno Marios Regional Park. The pioneer camp has been inactive since the 1990s and is slowly deteriorating. The buildings found there were overtaken by nature and are receding into the green background of the forest, disappearing into the dark of night as if they are in their last moments.
The ideas of this series were later expanded upon in Light of the Past (2022–2023).
